The Ark is a website created for a non-profit organization that provides shelter and support for rescued animals. The website features a clean and modern design, with easy navigation and informative content about the organization's mission, services, and how to get involved. Visitors can learn about the different types of animals that the organization helps, as well as the various ways they can contribute to the cause, such as volunteering or donating.
